Transition & Changes

Transition is a process of adapting to changes. Managing personal routines, lifestyle and learning journey is a process that prepares you for the future.



Studies suggest that heightened anxiety may predict a more challenging process to adapt to new environments. 



This is not always related to ability or academic skills, but could be influenced by

mood, anxiety, destructive thoughts

social stress

independent living skills



It is best to learn these skills early to enhance the ability to cope with changes.

Study skills (time management, revision techniques)

Adaptability (keep an open mind, build resilience rather than avoidance)

Emotional regulation (mindfulness, seek help/support)
